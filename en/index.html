 FAQ

What is FINANS PROTECT OCR?

An AI accounting assistant for your business: a service for classifying, extracting, validating and exporting data from invoices and accounting documents, reducing manual entry before accounting posting.

 FAQ

How accurate is OCR and when is human review used?

Accuracy depends on document quality, template and field visibility. The workflow combines OCR with UIC/VAT/IBAN checks, totals reconciliation and a review queue, so uncertain or conflicting documents are reviewed by a person before export.

 FAQ

Does it support Microinvest export?

Yes. The pipeline prepares data for Microinvest Sklad XML and Delta Kontirovki. During a pilot, we check the exact version, import settings and sample files before production use.

 FAQ

How are personal and company data handled?

The approach is self-hosted/GDPR-aligned: processing can run in controlled infrastructure with access limits and agreed retention. Documents are not used to train public models.

 FAQ

How does onboarding or a pilot start?

We start with a small set of real invoices, field mapping, test Microinvest/Delta export and a warnings report. Then we define intake channels, review roles, approval rules and production volumes.

 FAQ

What drives pricing?

Pricing depends on monthly document volume, template complexity, human review needs, intake channels, self-hosted requirements, API integrations and support level. A pilot with real documents validates the quote.

 FAQ

Which documents are supported?

The focus is Bulgarian invoices, PDFs, scans and readable photos. Similar accounting documents can be tested, while receipts, handwriting and unusual formats require a reliability check first.

 FAQ

How does it work for accounting offices?

Documents can be separated by client, channel and status. AI extracts and validates data, exceptions go to a review queue, and approved records are prepared for Microinvest/Delta export with an audit trail.

 FAQ

Is it ready for e-invoicing and the Bulgarian NRA?

We track e-invoicing and NRA-related requirements, but do not make unsupported legal claims. The product prepares structured data, validates fields and keeps processing evidence; legal compliance should be confirmed with an accountant or legal advisor.
